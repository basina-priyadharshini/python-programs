By Bus Traveling to Kedarnath

Traveling to Kedarnath by bus is one of the most economical and popular options for pilgrims. Government and private buses operate regularly up to Gaurikund, which is the last motorable point before Kedarnath. From Gaurikund, devotees must trek or use pony/palki services to reach the temple.
