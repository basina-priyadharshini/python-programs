Our Mission

The mission of APIIC focuses on promoting balanced and inclusive industrial development across the state. APIIC is committed to planning, developing, and managing industrial areas that meet global standards in infrastructure, connectivity, and sustainability. The corporation works to attract national and international investors by ensuring efficient processes, clear land allotment systems, and single-window support services. APIIC also aims to empower local entrepreneurs, MSMEs, and startups by providing them with necessary facilities and guidance.

Our Vision

The Andhra Pradesh Industrial Infrastructure Corporation (APIIC) envisions transforming Andhra Pradesh into a globally competitive industrial destination. Its vision emphasizes the development of world-class industrial infrastructure that can support advanced manufacturing, innovation, and sustainable economic growth. APIIC aims to create an ecosystem where industries can flourish with ease, supported by high-quality facilities, transparent governance, and investor-friendly policies.
