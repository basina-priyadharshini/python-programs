Agriculture in Gujarat

  • Major crops: Cotton, Groundnut, Bajra, Wheat, Rice, Sugarcane.
  • Fruit crops: Kesar Mango, Banana, Chickoo (Sapota), Dates.
  • Vegetables: Potato, Onion, Tomato, Brinjal.
  • Special area: Kutch famous for dates & hardy crops.
  • Uses latest irrigation technology like drip irrigation, sprinklers.
