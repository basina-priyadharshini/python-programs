SHIP REPAIR

CSL started offering ship repair services in 1982 and has undertaken upgrades and repairs for all types of ships including ships for the oil exploration industry. CSL has very rich experience in ship repair front and has successfully completed more than 2500 ship repair projects so far of clients from Defense and Commercial sectors. CSL has been closely associating with Indian Navy for undertaking refits and maintenance of their fleet since commencement of ship repairs. CSL is the only yard in India capable of undertaking dry dock repairs of Aircraft carriers owned by Indian Navy.CSL has successfully completed refits of INS Viraat,INS Vikramaditya & INS Vikrant. Apart from repairs/ refits, CSL has also undertaken upgradation of ships of oil exploration industry as well as periodical maintenance, life extension etc.

Geographical Expansion & Pan India Presence
