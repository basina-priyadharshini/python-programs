Find the answers to all your questions about tickets, rides, safety and more!

Are all the rides included in the entry ticket? +
Yes, all rides and attractions inside the park are included in the entry ticket unless mentioned otherwise.
Do you offer individual ride tickets? +
No, individual ride tickets are not available. All rides are included with the entry ticket.
Should I make reservations before coming? +
Online booking is recommended for faster entry, but tickets are also available at the counter.
What is the minimum age for full ticket entry? +
Children above a specified height are required to purchase a full ticket.
Are lockers or changing rooms available? +
Yes, lockers and changing rooms are available near water rides.
