Deity of Kedarnath

The deity of the Kedarnath Temple is Lord Shiva. He is worshipped here in the form of a Shiva Lingam. Kedarnath is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, which are considered the most sacred.

Lord Shiva is known as the Destroyer of Evil and the Protector of the Universe. Devotees believe that visiting Kedarnath helps in attaining peace, forgiveness of sins, and spiritual growth.
