Andhra Pradesh Industrial Infrastructure Corporation (APIIC)

The Andhra Pradesh Industrial Infrastructure Corporation (APIIC) is a government organization established to promote and support industrial development in Andhra Pradesh. It was formed in 1973 and functions as the main agency responsible for creating world -class industrial infrastructure in the state. APIIC acquires land, develops industrial areas, and provides all basic facilities required for industries to set up and operate smoothly.

APIIC plays a key role in attracting investments by developing industrial parks, Special Economic Zones (SEZs), industrial corridors, and sector-specific parks like food processing parks, textile parks, electronics manufacturing clusters, and more. These areas are equipped with essential infrastructure such as roads, water supply, power, drainage, communication networks, and ready-to-use factory sheds.

To make the process easier for businesses, APIIC also acts as a facilitator between industries and the government. It helps entrepreneurs receive approvals, land allotments, and other required permissions quickly. In many industrial areas, APIIC functions as a local authority, maintaining streetlights, sanitation, water pipelines, and basic civic services.

Over the years, APIIC has contributed significantly to the economic growth of the state by creating employment opportunities, supporting small and large industries, and ensuring balanced industrial development across all districts.
