Administration of Kedarnath

Temple Management

The Kedarnath Temple is managed by the Badrinath-Kedarnath Temple Committee (BKTC). The committee oversees daily rituals, temple maintenance, and pilgrimage arrangements.

Role of Government

The Uttarakhand Government supports temple administration by providing security, infrastructure, disaster management, and medical facilities.

Responsibilities
  • Conducting daily pujas and rituals
  • Managing donations and funds
  • Maintaining cleanliness
  • Helping pilgrims
  • Coordination with authorities
Security & Crowd Control

Security is handled by police, disaster response teams, and local authorities to ensure safe pilgrimage during peak Char Dham Yatra seasons.
