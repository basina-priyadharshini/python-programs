Kedarnath map

Kedarnath is a famous Hindu pilgrimage place located in the Himalayas of Uttarakhand, India. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. The Kedarnath Temple is situated near the Mandakini River and is surrounded by snow-covered mountains. Thousands of devotees visit Kedarnath every year, especially during the Char Dham Yatra. The temple is open only from April/May to October/November due to heavy snowfall in winter.
