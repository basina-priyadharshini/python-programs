About us at Shipcon Enterprises

Shipcon Enterprise has been started by people with extensive Shipbuilding and Ship Repair Experience which includes Naval Architects and Engineers. With vast experience in building and repair of numerous vessel types, Shipcon has proved in this short time frame that it will soon emerge as one of the prime Shipbuilders and Ship Repair Yards of India.

We at SHIPCON, have the expertise in building various Inland Vessels, Coastal Vessels and Sea Going Vessels up to 80 meters in length. The experience comes from the Top Management of the company having worked in various renowned Shipbuilders and Ship Design companies of India in senior positions.

We want to remain in the small Vessel segment of Ships and Boats up to 80 M in length.
