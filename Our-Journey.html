APIIC – Our Journey

Andhra Pradesh Industrial Infrastructure Corporation (APIIC) has played a pivotal role in shaping the industrial landscape of Andhra Pradesh. Since its establishment, APIIC has focused on developing world-class infrastructure, enabling investments, and creating employment opportunities across the state.

•
1973
Andhra Pradesh Industrial Infrastructure Corporation (APIIC) was established to promote industrial development across the state.
•
1980
APIIC expanded major industrial estates and created planned industrial layouts with essential infrastructure.
•
1992
APIIC became a key facilitator of Public–Private Partnerships (PPP) and attracted national & international industries.
•
2000
Supported the development of HITEC City expansions and strengthened the IT sector growth in Andhra Pradesh.
•
2005
Launched Special Economic Zones (SEZs) including IT/ITES SEZs, Pharma SEZ, and Multiproduct SEZs.
•
2010
Started major Industrial Corridor Development aligned with national initiatives.
•
2014
Post-bifurcation, APIIC reorganized and focused on new industrial planning for Andhra Pradesh.
•
2017
Developed Mega Industrial Parks like Sri City Expansion & Orvakal Mega Industrial Hub.
•
2020
Introduced Single Desk System for transparent, fast industrial approvals.
•
2023
Led development under VCIC & CBIC industrial corridors, boosting ports, MSME zones & logistics hubs.
