Kedarnath Temple Offerings

Abhishekam

Offering of water, milk, and honey to Lord Shiva during pooja rituals.

Bilva Patra

Sacred Bilva leaves offered to Lord Shiva for blessings and peace.

Deepa & Aarti

Lighting of lamps and performing aarti as an act of devotion.

Prasadam

Sacred food offerings distributed to devotees after pooja.

Donations

Monetary offerings used for temple maintenance and pilgrim services.

Special Pooja Offerings

Offerings made during Rudrabhishekam and other special rituals.
