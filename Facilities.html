Accommodation

For pilgrims visiting Kedarnath, government guest houses (GMVN),
dharamshalas, private lodges, and temporary tents are available.
During peak yatra season, advance booking is recommended.
Basic facilities such as beds, blankets, hot water, and toilets are provided for pilgrims.

Food Facilities

Pilgrims are provided with free langar services, annadanam centers,
and small hotels and food stalls.
The food served is generally simple, hygienic, and vegetarian.
Safe drinking water facilities are also available along the yatra route.

Medical Support

During the yatra season, medical camps, first-aid centers,
and emergency health services are arranged.
Doctors, paramedical staff, oxygen cylinders, and basic medicines are available.
Regular health check-ups help pilgrims cope with high-altitude conditions.

Pony & Palki

For elderly pilgrims, children, and physically challenged devotees,
pony, mule, and palki services are available.
These services are provided by registered operators and follow safety guidelines,
making the trek easier and safer.

Security

To ensure pilgrim safety, police, SDRF, and disaster
management teams are deployed round the clock.
Crowd control, weather monitoring, CCTV surveillance, and
emergency rescue servicesare arranged throughout the yatra route.
