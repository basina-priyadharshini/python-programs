RTI ACT


RTI Section 4(1)(b)(i):
The Right to Information (RTI) Act empowers citizens to seek information from public authorities,
promoting transparency and accountability
Public Information Officers:
The Public Information Officer (PIO) is responsible for handling and responding to RTI requests,
ensuring the information is provided in a timely and accurate manner.
