Interview Q & A

Q: What motivates you every day?
Helping people and seeing hope in their eyes motivates me more than awards or fame.
Q: How do you handle criticism?
I listen, learn, and move forward. Criticism helps you grow if taken positively.
Q: Advice for young generation?
Be disciplined, respect time, and never lose humanity in the race for success.
